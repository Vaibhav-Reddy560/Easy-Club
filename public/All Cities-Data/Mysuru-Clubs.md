**Institutional and Community Club Ecosystems of Mysuru: A Comprehensive Socio-Technical Analysis**  
The city of Mysuru, historically celebrated as the "Sandalwood City" and the cultural capital of Karnataka, has evolved into a sophisticated hub of educational excellence and civic engagement. This evolution is driven by a diverse array of organizations that cater to the intellectual, creative, and social aspirations of its residents. The ecosystem is defined by a distinct duality: the presence of high-century academic institutions like the University of Mysore (UoM) and the National Institute of Engineering (NIE), alongside a vibrant non-collegiate sector comprising legacy social clubs, global NGOs, and modern creative hubs.
